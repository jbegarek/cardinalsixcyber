Most small businesses do not have a tooling problem first. They have a prioritization problem.

## Start With Access And Accountability

If you cannot explain who has access, why they have it, and how you review that access, your next purchase probably will not solve the underlying control gap.

## Build A Small Repeatable Control Set

- Tighten privileged access
- Review asset inventory
- Establish log review ownership
- Document how exceptions are approved

## What Good Looks Like

A small business does not need enterprise ceremony. It needs repeatable decisions, visible ownership, and enough documentation to survive turnover and assessment pressure.
