Security programs often fail at the handoff between theory and operator behavior.

## Why This Matters

Research is useful only when it changes how an organization chooses, sequences, and defends controls under real constraints.

## Operator Takeaway

If a control decision cannot be defended in plain language, the implementation is probably too brittle for real-world assessment pressure.
