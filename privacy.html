Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Cardinal Six Cyber LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects minimal information necessary to provide our cybersecurity consulting services. When you contact us via email, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your organization name, if provided
  • The content of your message

We do not use cookies for tracking. Our website does not include third-party analytics, advertising scripts, or social media tracking pixels. Cloudflare provides basic traffic analytics as part of our hosting infrastructure.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through contact forms or direct email is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide the consulting services you request
  • Maintain records of our business relationship

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

As a cybersecurity firm, we take data protection seriously. Your information is protected through encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), access controls, and secure handling practices consistent with the same standards we advise our clients to implement.

Data Retention

We retain contact information and correspondence for the duration of our business relationship plus a reasonable period thereafter for legal and operational purposes. You may request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Our website is served through Cloudflare, which may collect basic access logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of its content delivery and security services. Cloudflare's privacy policy governs their handling of this data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at info@cardinalsixcyber.com.
